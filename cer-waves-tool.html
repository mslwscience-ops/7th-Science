Step 2 of 4 — Data Collection

Record Your Observations

Run the simulation at at least 5 different frequency settings. Record the frequency and use the ruler to measure the wavelength each time. Keep everything else the same (controlled variables).

How to measure wavelength Wavelength = the distance from one wave crest to the next crest. Use the on-screen ruler. Pause the sim if it helps.

Step 3 of 4 — Write Your CER

Claim · Evidence · Reasoning

● Claim
What is a Claim? A one-sentence answer to the research question. Do NOT say "I think" or "I believe." State it as a fact based on your data.
● Evidence
What is Evidence? Specific data from your experiment — numbers, measurements, observations. Reference your data table. More than one data point is stronger.
● Reasoning
What is Reasoning? Explain the science behind your evidence. Connect your data to a scientific principle or law. Why does this happen?
